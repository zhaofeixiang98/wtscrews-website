Tentang WT Fastener

Our Story

We started back in 2005 — just a small workshop, a tanganful of machines, and a sederhana idea: make fastener that perform exactly as promised, every single time.

Over the past two decades we have grown into a full-scale operation with CNC machining, cold forging lines, and automated quality inspection. But the core idea has not changed. Every fastener that leaves our factory goes through the same rigorous checks we would expect if we were the ones using them.

Today we ship to over 50 countries. Our katalog covers thousands of spesifikasis — standar hex bolts, flat washers, kustom-engineered solutions for automotive, konstruksi, and renewable energi projects. We hold ISO 9001 sertifikasi and stand behind every product we deliver.

Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom pesanan, the MOQ may vary depending on spesifikasis — feel gratis to ask.

How long does produksi take?

Standar products: 7–15 business hari. Custom pesanan: 15–25 business hari depending on complexity and quantity.

Can you send sampels before I place a massa pesanan?

Yes. We provide gratis sampels for standar items (you cover pengiriman). For kustom parts, a small sampel fee may apply.

What pembayaran methods do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small pesanan. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Do you offer OEM / kustom manufacturing?

Absolutely. Kirim us your gambars or spesifikasis and we will provide a penawaran within 24 hours.
