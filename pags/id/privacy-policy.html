Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom pesanan, the MOQ may vary depending on spesifikasis — feel gratis to ask.

Do you offer gratis sampels?

Yes — we provide gratis sampels for standar products. Freight is typically covered by the buyer, or we can deduct it from your first pesanan.

What sertifikasis do you have?

Our factory is ISO 9001 certified. We can penawaran material certificates, test laporans, and certificates of conformity with every shipment.

How long is your waktu produksi?

Standar items ship in 3–7 hari. Custom pesanan typically take 15–30 hari depending on quantity and spesifikasis.

Can you do OEM / kustom kemasan?

Absolutely. We dukungan kustom branding, kemasan, and labeling to match your requirements.
