Circlip

Our circlips (also known as retaining rings or snap rings) are engineered fasteners that fit into a groove on a shaft or in a bore to retain components such as bearings, gears, and other machine elements.

Available in both external (for shafts) and internal (for bores) types. Manufactured to DIN 471 and DIN 472 standards with high-quality spring steel or stainless steel.
