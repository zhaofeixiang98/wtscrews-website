Jangkar Drop-In

Our drop-in anchors are internally berulir anchors designed for use in concrete. They are flush-mounted anchors that provide a secure ulir for attaching bolts, berulir rod, or other fastener. The anchor expands into the concrete as the setting tool is used.

Also known as machine screw anchors or female anchors. Available in seng-selaput karbon baja for indoor use and nirkarat baja for outdoor and corrosive lingkungans.
