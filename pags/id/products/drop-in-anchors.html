Anchor Drop-In

Our drop-in anchors are internally threaded anchors designed for use in concrete. They are flush-mounted anchors that provide a secure thread for attaching bolts, threaded rod, or other fasteners. The anchor expands into the concrete as the setting tool is used.

Also known as machine screw anchors or female anchors. Available in zinc-plated carbon steel for indoor use and stainless steel for outdoor and corrosive environments.
