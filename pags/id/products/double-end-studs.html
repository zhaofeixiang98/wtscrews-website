Stud Ujung Ganda

Our double end studs (also known as stud bolts or b studs) feature threading at both ends with an unthreaded center section. They are essential for flanged connections in piping systems, pressure vessels, and heat exchangers.

We manufacture to DIN 938 (2d thread length) and DIN 939 (2.5d thread length) standards. Available in various materials including stainless steel, carbon steel, and alloy steel.
