Stud Ujung Ganda

Our double end studs (also known as stud bolts or b studs) feature uliring at both ends with an unberulir center section. They are essential for flanged connections in piping systems, tekanan vessels, and heat exchangers.

We manufacture to DIN 938 (2d ulir panjang) and DIN 939 (2.5d ulir panjang) standar. Available in various materials including nirkarat baja, karbon baja, and alloy baja.
