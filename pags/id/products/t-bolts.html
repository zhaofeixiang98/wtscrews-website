Baut T

Our T bolts are specifically designed for T-slot aluminum profiles used in modular framing systems, machine guards, and automation equipment. The T-shaped head fits snugly into the profile slot.

Available in various sizes to match standard T-slot profiles. Compatible with most European and Asian profile systems.
