Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What is the difference between serrated and non-serrated flange nuts?

Serrated flange nuts have teeth on the bearing surface that bite into the mating material, providing excellent vibration resistance. Non-serrated versions rely on the washer face for beban distribution only.

Are these compatible with flange bolts?

Yes, flange nuts are designed to work with standar hex bolts and flange bolts. The integrated washer face distributes beban evenly.

What surface finishes do you offer?

We provide Zinc Plated, Hot-Dip Galvanized, and Black Oxide finishes.

Can you penawaran different flange diameters?

Yes, we can produce flange nuts with different flange diameters per your spesifikasis.
