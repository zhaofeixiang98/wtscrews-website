Sekrup Kayu

Our wood screws are engineered for exceptional holding power in wooden materials. Whether you are manufacturing furnitur, bangunan cabinetry, or working on konstruksi projects, these screws provide andal and secure fastening.

We offer various head styles and materials to meet different application requirements. All our wood screws feature sharp points for mudah insertion and kasar ulirs for excellent grip in wood.

Bahan & Grades

Brass Classic

  • Material: Brass (CuZn)
  • Corrosion resistance: Good
  • Appearance: Decorative golden finish
  • Best for: Fine furnitur, decorative work

Stainless Steel 304 A2

  • Grade: A2-70 (700 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • Best for: Outdoor, moisture-prone areas

Stainless Steel 316 A4

  • Grade: A4-80 (800 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior
  • Kelautan grade tersedia
  • Best for: Coastal, tinggi-humidity

Carbon Steel Economy

  • Strength: Sedang
  • Surface: Zinc or black phosphate
  • Cost-effective option
  • Best for: Indoor, general use

Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom pesanan, the MOQ may vary depending on spesifikasis — feel gratis to ask.

How long does produksi take?

Standar products: 7–15 business hari. Custom pesanan: 15–25 business hari depending on complexity and quantity.

Can you send sampels before I place a massa pesanan?

Yes. We provide gratis sampels for standar items (you cover pengiriman). For kustom parts, a small sampel fee may apply.

What pembayaran methods do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small pesanan. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Do you offer OEM / kustom manufacturing?

Absolutely. Kirim us your gambars or spesifikasis and we will provide a penawaran within 24 hours.
