Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 sets per item. For kustom kits, the MOQ may vary depending on contents — feel gratis to ask.

Can I kustomize the kit contents?

Yes, we offer fully kustomizable kits. Just tell us what items and quantities you need, and we will prepare a penawaran.

How long does produksi take?

Standar kits: 7–15 business hari. Custom kits: 15–25 business hari depending on complexity and quantity.

What pembayaran methods do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small pesanan. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Do you offer OEM kemasan with our brand?

Absolutely. We can kustomize kemasan with your logo and branding. Kontak us for more details.
