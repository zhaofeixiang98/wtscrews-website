Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

Can spring washers be stacked?

Yes, Belleville washers can be stacked in parallel or series to achieve different spring rates and beban capacities.

What is the temperature range?

Carbon baja: -30°C to +120°C. Stainless baja: -200°C to +300°C. 50CrV4: up to +210°C.

What is the difference between Series A and B?

Series A is for stacked applications (bearing bebans). Series B is for single use (low deflection).

Do you provide kustom ukuran?

Yes, we can manufacture kustom ukuran and spesifikasis per your gambars.
