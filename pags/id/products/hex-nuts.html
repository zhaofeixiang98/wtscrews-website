Bahan & Grade

Carbon Baja Grade 8

  • Class 8 (8 MPa proof load)
  • Paling umum, tujuan umum
  • Dilapisi seng untuk ketahanan korosi
  • Compatible with Grade 8.8 bolts

Carbon Baja Grade 10

  • Class 10 (10 MPa proof load)
  • Kekuatan lebih tinggi
  • For high-strength applications
  • Compatible with Grade 10.9 bolts

Stainless Baja A2

  • A2 (Baja Tahan Karat 304)
  • Tahan korosi
  • Non-magnetis
  • Kompatibel grade makanan

Stainless Baja A4

  • A4 (Baja Tahan Karat 316)
  • Grade laut
  • Tahan klorida
  • Luar/ruang applications

Pertanyaan yang Sering Diajukan

Berapa jumlah pesanan minimum?

MOQ standar kami adalah 10 kg per item. Untuk pesanan khusus, MOQ dapat bervariasi tergantung spesifikasi — silakan tanyakan.

What grades do you offer?

We offer Grade 4, 6, 8, and 10 for carbon steel, and A2 (304) and A4 (316) for stainless steel.

What surface finishes are available?

We provide berlapis seng, galvanis celup panas, oksida hitam, and pasivasi finishes.

Can you supply prevailing torque nuts?

Yes, we offer prevailing torque type (Nyloc style) hex nuts upon request.

Do you provide test certificates?

Yes, all shipments include material test certificates and quality inspection reports.
