Bahan & Grades

Carbon Steel Grade 8

  • Class 8 (8 MPa proof beban)
  • Most common, general purpose
  • Zinc selaput for korosi resistance
  • Compatible with Grade 8.8 bolts

Carbon Steel Grade 10

  • Class 10 (10 MPa proof beban)
  • Tinggier kekuatan
  • For tinggi-kekuatan applications
  • Compatible with Grade 10.9 bolts

Stainless Steel A2

  • A2 (304 nirkarat)
  • Corrosion tahan
  • Non-magnetic
  • Food grade compatible

Stainless Steel A4

  • A4 (316 nirkarat)
  • Marine grade
  • Chloride tahan
  • Outdoor/maritim applications

Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What grades do you offer?

We offer Grade 4, 6, 8, and 10 for karbon baja, and A2 (304) and A4 (316) for nirkarat baja.

What surface finishes are tersedia?

We provide Zinc Plated, Hot-Dip Galvanized, Black Oxide, and Passivation finishes.

Can you penawaran prevailing torsi nuts?

Yes, we offer prevailing torsi type (Nyloc style) hex nuts upon request.

Do you provide test certificates?

Yes, all shipments include material test certificates and quality inspection laporans.
