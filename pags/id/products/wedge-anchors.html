Jangkar Baji

Our anchor baji are heavy-duty expansion anchors designed for use in solid concrete. The anchor expands against the concrete as the bolt is tightened, providing excellent holding power for heavy bebans.

Available in seng-selaput karbon baja for indoor use and nirkarat baja (304/316) for outdoor and corrosive lingkungans. Tested and approved for seismic applications.
