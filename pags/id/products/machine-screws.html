Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What head types are tersedia?

We offer Pan Head, Countersunk (Flat), Oval, Button, and Hex Head machine screws.

What drive types do you offer?

We provide Slotted, Phillips, Pozi, Torx, and Hex (Socket) drive types.

Can you produce kustom panjangs?

Yes, we can produce machine screws in kustom panjangs from 3mm to 100mm per your spesifikasis.

What grades are tersedia?

We offer Carbon Steel Grade 4.8 and 8.8, and SS A2 (304) and A4 (316).
