Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

How many times can a Nyloc nut be reused?

Nyloc nuts can typically be reused 5-10 times while maintaining adequate locking torsi, depending on the application and ulir condition.

What is the temperature range for Nyloc nuts?

Standar nylon insert: -40°C to +120°C. For tinggi-temperature applications, we offer metal insert lock nuts (prevailing torsi nuts).

Can Nyloc nuts be used with nirkarat baja bolts?

Yes, but we recommend using the same material grade to avoid galling. Stainless baja Nyloc nuts are ideal for use with nirkarat baja bolts.

Do you offer thin profile Nyloc nuts?

Yes, we offer both full tinggi and thin profile versions per DIN 985 and ISO 10511 spesifikasis.
