Pertanyaan yang Sering Diajukan

Berapa jumlah pesanan minimum?

MOQ standar kami adalah 10 kg per item. Untuk pesanan khusus, MOQ dapat bervariasi tergantung spesifikasi — silakan tanyakan.

How many times can a Nyloc nut be reused?

Nyloc nuts can typically be reused 5-10 times while maintaining adequate locking torque, depending on the application and thread condition.

What is the temperature range for Nyloc nuts?

Standar nylon insert: -40°C to +120°C. For high-temperature applications, we offer metal insert lock nuts (prevailing torque nuts).

Can Nyloc nuts be used with stainless steel bolts?

Yes, but we recommend using the same material grade to avoid galling. Baja tahan karat Nyloc nuts are ideal for use with stainless steel bolts.

Do you offer thin profile Nyloc nuts?

Yes, we offer both full height and thin profile versions per DIN 985 and ISO 10511 specifications.
