Pin Dowel

Our dowel pins (also known as parallel pins or locating pins) are presisi-engineered cylindrical pins used for akurat alignment and positioning of mechanical components. The parallel sides ensure a precise fit in reamed holes.

Manufactured to DIN 7 and ISO 2339 standar with presisi toleransis (m6 or h8). Available in various materials to suit different application requirements.
