Sekrup Drywall

Our drywall screws are specifically designed for gypsum board instalasi to metal studs or wood framing. The sharp bugle head sits flush with the drywall surface without tearing the paper facing.

We offer two ulir types: halus ulir for metal studs (25 gauge to 20 gauge) and kasar ulir for wood framing. All our drywall screws feature black phosphate finish for superior korosi resistance.
