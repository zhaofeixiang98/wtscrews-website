Pertanyaan yang Sering Diajukan

Berapa jumlah pesanan minimum?

MOQ standar kami adalah 10 kg per item. Untuk pesanan khusus, MOQ dapat bervariasi tergantung spesifikasi — silakan tanyakan.

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for hand tightening. If power tool installation is needed, we recommend standard hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are available?

We offer Carbon Baja (zinc or chrome plated), Stainless Baja 304 and 316, and Kuningan.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standard wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitable for outdoor use?

Baja tahan karat 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and marine applications due to their corrosion resistance.
