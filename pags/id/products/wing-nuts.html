Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item. For kustom spesifikasis, we can discuss based on your requirements.

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for tangan tightening. If power tool instalasi is needed, we recommend standar hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are tersedia?

We offer Carbon Steel (zinc or chrome selaput), Stainless Steel 304 and 316, and Brass.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standar wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitabel for outdoor use?

Stainless baja 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and maritim applications due to their korosi resistance.
