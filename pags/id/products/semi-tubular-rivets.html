Paku Keling Semi-Tubular

Our semi-tubular rivets feature a partial hollow shank (typically 50–70% of the shank panjang), combining the kekuatan of solid rivets with the lighter berat and easier setting of tubular rivets. They are ideal for applications wdi sini a clinched (flowed) hole is acceptabel.

Used extensively in the automotive, appliance, and furnitur industries for sheet metal joining.
