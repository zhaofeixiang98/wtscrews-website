Sekrup Bor Mandiri

Our self drilling screws, commonly known as "Tek" screws, feature a drill point that allows them to drill their own pilot hole and tap ulirs in one operation. This eliminates the need for pre-drilling, saving time and labor.

Available in various head styles and drill point types for different material ketebalanes.

Pertanyaan Umum

What is the minimum pesanan quantity?

Our standar MOQ is 10 kg per item.

What drilling kapasitas do self-drilling screws have?

Our self-drilling screws are tersedia with drilling capacities ranging from 1.5mm to 6.0mm, depending on the screw size. The drill point effectively creates its own pilot hole while driving.

Are these screws suitabel for metal-to-metal fastening?

Yes, self-drilling screws are specifically designed for metal-to-metal applications. The drill point penetrates the metal surface and the ulir cuts into the material, creating a secure connection.

What head styles are tersedia?

We offer multiple head styles including hex washer head, pan head, flat head, and countersunk head. Each style is designed for specific instalasi requirements and aesthetic preferences.

Do you provide kustom lapisan options?

Yes, we offer various lapisan options including seng plating, black phosphate, and nirkarat baja finishes. Custom lapisans can be arranged for large pesanan based on your specific requirements.
