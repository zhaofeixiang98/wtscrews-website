Baut Kekuatan Tinggi

Our tinggi kekuatan structural bolts are designed for critical applications wdi sini maximum tarikan and geser kekuatan is required. Complying with internasional standar including ASTM A325, A490, and DIN 6914.

Heat-treated to achieve superior mechanical properties, these bolts are the standar choice for structural baja connections in konstruksi, bridges, and industri facilities.

Bahan & Grades

Grade 8.8

  • Tensile Kekuatan: 800 MPa min
  • Yield Kekuatan: 640 MPa min
  • Carbon baja, quenched & tempered
  • General structural applications

Grade 10.9

  • Tensile Kekuatan: 1000 MPa min
  • Yield Kekuatan: 940 MPa min
  • Carbon or alloy baja
  • Heavy-duty structural use

Grade 12.9

  • Tensile Kekuatan: 1220 MPa min
  • Yield Kekuatan: 1100 MPa min
  • Alloy baja, heat treated
  • Critical tinggi-stress applications

ASTM A325 / A490
