Baut Jangkar

Our anchor bolts provide andal fastening in concrete, brick, and masonry substrates. We offer multiple anchor technologies to match your beban requirements and instalasi conditions.

All anchor bolts are tested for pull-out kekuatan, geser kapasitas, and korosi resistance to ensure andal performance in permintaaning lingkungans.
