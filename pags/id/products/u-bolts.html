Baut U

Our U-bolts are specifically designed for clamping and mounting pipes, tubes, and cylindrical objects. The U-shaped design wraps around the pipe while the berulir ends allow for secure fastening with nuts.

Available in both square bend and round bend configurations to meet different instalasi requirements. We offer various materials including karbon baja and nirkarat baja.
