Pin Cotter

Our cotter pins (also known as split pins or R-pins) are simple but essential fasteners used to secure nuts, bolts, and other hardware in place. The split design provides spring tension to hold the pin in place after insertion.

Manufactured to DIN 94 and ISO 1234 standards. Available in various materials including stainless steel and carbon steel with different finishes.
