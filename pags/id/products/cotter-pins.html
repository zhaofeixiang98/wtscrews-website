Pin Cotter

Our cotter pins (also known as split pins or R-pins) are sederhana but essential fastener used to secure nuts, bolts, and other hardware in place. The split design provides spring tegangan to hold the pin in place after insertion.

Manufactured to DIN 94 and ISO 1234 standar. Available in various materials including nirkarat baja and karbon baja with different finishes.
