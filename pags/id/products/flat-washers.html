Pertanyaan yang Sering Diajukan

Berapa jumlah pesanan minimum?

MOQ standar kami adalah 10 kg per item. Untuk pesanan khusus, MOQ dapat bervariasi tergantung spesifikasi — silakan tanyakan.

What is the difference between Form A and Form B washers?

Form A is standard (un-chamfered), while Form B has chamfered edges. Both are available per DIN 125.

What hardness range do you offer?

Standar carbon steel washers have hardness HV 140-250. Higher hardness options available upon request.

Can you supply large diameter washers?

Ya, kami bisa produce washers up to M64 and custom large diameters as per your specifications.

Do you offer special finishes?

Yes, we offer berlapis seng, galvanis celup panas, oksida hitam, and pasivasi finishes.
