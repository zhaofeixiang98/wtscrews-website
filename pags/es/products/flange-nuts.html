Preguntas Frecuentes

¿Cuál es la cantidad mínima de pedido?

Nuestro pedido mínimo estándar es de 10 kg por artículo. Para pedidos personalizados, el mínimo puede variar según las especificaciones — no dude en consultar.

What is the difference between serrated and non-serrated flange nuts?

Serrated flange nuts have teeth on the bearing surface that bite into the mating material, providing excellent vibration resistance. Non-serrated versions rely on the washer face for load distribution only.

Are these compatible with flange bolts?

Yes, flange nuts are designed to work with standard hex bolts and flange bolts. The integrated washer face distributes load evenly.

What surface finishes do you offer?

Ofrecemos galvanizado, galvanizado en caliente y óxido negro.

Can you supply different flange diameters?

Sí, podemos produce flange nuts with different flange diameters per your specifications.
