Preguntas Frecuentes

¿Cuál es la cantidad mínima de pedido?

Nuestro pedido mínimo estándar es de 10 kg por artículo. Para pedidos personalizados, el mínimo puede variar según las especificaciones — no dude en consultar.

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for hand tightening. If power tool installation is needed, we recommend standard hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are available?

We offer Carbon Acero (zinc or chrome plated), Stainless Acero 304 and 316, and Latón.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standard wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitable for outdoor use?

Acero inoxidable 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and marine applications due to their corrosion resistance.
