螺杆

Our threaded rods (also known as all-thread or stud bolts) provide full-length threading along the entire shaft. They are versatile fasteners used for anchoring, suspending, and connecting in various industrial applications.

可选 in metric and imperial sizes with various thread pitches. We offer both left-hand and right-hand thread options for specialized applications.
