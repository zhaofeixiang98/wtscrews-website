材质与等级

不锈钢304A2

  • 等级：A2-70（700 MPa）
  • 耐腐蚀性：优异
  • 温度范围：-196°C 至 +800°C
  • Best for: Indoor, food industry

不锈钢316A4

  • 等级：A4-80（800 MPa）
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior
  • Marine grade available
  • Best for: Outdoor, marine

碳钢8.8

  • Grade: 8.8 (800 MPa)
  • Surface: Zinc or galvanized
  • Cost-effective option
  • Best for: General industrial

合金钢10.9

  • Grade: 10.9 (1040 MPa)
  • High strength option
  • Black oxide finish
  • Best for: Heavy duty
