多种材质和等级可选，适用于不同应用s to match your bolt requirements. Full traceability and quality certification available.

  • Class 8 (8 MPa proof load)
  • Most common, general purpose
  • Zinc plated for corrosion resistance

Carbon Steel Grade 10

  • Chloride resistant

常见问题

最小订购量是多少？

我们的标准最小订购量为每规格10kg。定制订单的MOQ因规格和数量而异

What grades do you offer?

我们提供碳钢4、6、8、10级可选, and A2 (304) and A4 (316) for stainless steel.

What surface finishes are available?

我们提供镀锌、热镀锌、黑色氧化和达克罗涂层ed, Black Oxide, and Passivation finishes.

Can you supply prevailing torque nuts?

是的，我们提供有效力矩型（尼龙锁紧）和全金属锁紧螺母loc style) hex nuts upon request.

Do you provide test certificates?

是的，所有发货都附有材料检测证书 and quality inspection reports.
