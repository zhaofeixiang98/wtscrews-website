材质与等级

碳钢

  • Grade 4.8, 8.8, 10.9
  • Good strength, cost-effective
  • Zinc plated for corrosion resistance

不锈钢304

  • A2-70, A2-80
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • Suitable for indoor/outdoor use

不锈钢316

  • A4-70, A4-80
  • Superior marine corrosion resistance
  • Chloride and acid resistant

热镀锌

  • Heavy-duty corrosion protection
  • Suitable for outdoor applications
  • Extended service life

常见问题

最小订购量是多少？

我们的标准最小订购量为每规格10kg。定制订单的MOQ因规格和数量而异

生产需要多长时间？

标准产品：7-15个工作日。定制产品：15-25个工作日 depending on specifications.

What materials are available for anchor bolts?

我们提供碳钢（4.8、8.8、10.9级）、合金钢和不锈钢0.9), Stainless Steel 304 (A2-70/80), and Stainless Steel 316 (A4-70/80).

Do you provide installation guidance?

Yes, we provide detailed installation guides and technical support for all our anchor products.

Can you provide custom anchor bolt designs?

Absolutely! 我们可以按您的精确规格生产锚栓 specific drawings and specifications. Contact us with your requirements.
