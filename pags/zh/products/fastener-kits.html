Our standard 最小订购量 is 10 sets per item. For 定制 kits, the 最小订购量 may vary depending on contents — feel free to ask.

Yes, we offer fully 定制izable kits. Just tell us what items and quantities you need, and we will prepare a quote.

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small orders. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Absolutely. 我们可以 定制ize packaging with your logo and branding. 联系 us for more details.
