楔形锚栓

Our wedge anchors are heavy-duty expansion anchors designed for use in solid concrete. The anchor expands against the concrete as the bolt is tightened, providing excellent holding power for heavy loads.

可选 in zinc-plated carbon steel for indoor use and stainless steel (304/316) for outdoor and corrosive environments. Tested and approved for seismic applications.
