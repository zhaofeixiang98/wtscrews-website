楔形锚栓

我们的楔形锚栓是重型膨胀螺栓 designed for use in solid concrete. The anchor expands against the concrete as the bolt is tightened, providing excellent holding power for heavy loads.

镀锌碳钢适用于室内，热镀锌适用于户外 use and stainless steel (304/316) for outdoor and corrosive environments. Tested and approved for seismic applications.
