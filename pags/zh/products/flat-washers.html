Washer Pipih

Our flat washers are the most common type of washer, used to distribute the load of a bolt or nut over a larger surface area. This prevents leakage, loosening, and surface damage.

Manufactured to DIN 125 / ISO 7089 standards with precise dimensional accuracy and consistent thickness.

常见问题

最小订购量是多少？

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What is the difference between Form A and Form B washers?

Form A is standard (un-chamfered), while Form B has chamfered edges. Both are available per DIN 125.

What hardness range do you offer?

Standard carbon steel washers have hardness HV 140-250. Higher hardness options available upon request.

Can you supply large diameter washers?

Yes, we can produce washers up to M64 and custom large diameters as per your specifications.

Do you offer special finishes?

Yes, we offer Zinc Plated, Hot-Dip Galvanized, Black Oxide, and Passivation finishes.
