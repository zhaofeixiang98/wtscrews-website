Washer Kunci

Our split lock washers feature a spring-like action that provides constant resilience against loosening caused by vibration. The split in the washer allows it to compress and spring back, maintaining tension on the fastener.

Standard in industrial and commercial applications worldwide. The teeth bite into the bearing surface for additional locking.

常见问题

最小订购量是多少？

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What is the difference between Type A and Type B lock washers?

Type A is the standard split lock washer. Type B has external teeth for additional locking in high-vibration applications.

What hardness are your lock washers?

Our standard carbon steel lock washers have hardness HV 440-510, providing optimal spring tension.

Can lock washers be reused?

Yes, lock washers can be reused several times, but the spring tension may decrease with each use.

Do you offer high-temperature lock washers?

Yes, we offer special high-temperature variants for applications above 200°C. Contact us for details.
