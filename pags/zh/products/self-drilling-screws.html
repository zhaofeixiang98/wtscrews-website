自钻螺钉

Our self drilling screws, commonly known as "Tek" screws, feature a drill point that allows them to drill their own pilot hole and tap threads in one operation. This eliminates the need for pre-drilling, saving time and labor.

可选 in various head styles and drill point types for different material thicknesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item.

What drilling capacity do self-drilling screws have?

Our self-drilling screws are available with drilling capacities ranging from 1.5mm to 6.0mm, depending on the screw size. The drill point effectively creates its own pilot hole while driving.

Are these screws suitable for metal-to-metal fastening?

是, self-drilling screws are specifically designed for metal-to-metal applications. The drill point penetrates the metal surface and the thread cuts into the material, creating a secure connection.

What head styles are available?

We offer multiple head styles including hex washer head, pan head, flat head, and countersunk head. 每个 style is designed for specific installation requirements and aesthetic preferences.

Do you provide custom coating options?

是, we offer various coating options including zinc plating, black phosphate, and stainless steel finishes. 定制 coatings can be arranged for large orders based on your specific requirements.
