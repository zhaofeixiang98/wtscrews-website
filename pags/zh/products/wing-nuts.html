常见问题

最小订购量是多少？

我们的标准最小订购量为每规格10kg。定制订单的MOQ因规格和数量而异

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for hand tightening. If power tool installation is needed, we recommend standard hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are available?

We offer Carbon Steel (zinc or chrome plated), Stainless Steel 304 and 316, and Brass.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standard wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitable for outdoor use?

Stainless steel 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and marine applications due to their corrosion resistance.
