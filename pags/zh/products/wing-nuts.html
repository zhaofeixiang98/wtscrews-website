Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard 最小订购量 is 10 kg per item. For 定制 specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for hand tightening. If power tool installation is needed, we recommend standard hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are 可提供?

我们提供 碳钢 (zinc or chrome plated), 不锈钢 304 and 316, and 黄铜.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standard wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitable for outdoor use?

不锈钢 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and marine applications due to their corrosion resistance.
