材质与等级

不锈钢304 A2

  • 等级: A2-70 (700 兆帕)
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • 最佳 for: Food, chemical, marine environments

不锈钢316 A4

  • 等级: A4-80 (800 兆帕)
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior (pitting resistant)
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • 最佳 for: Chloride, acidic environments

Carbon Steel 级别

  • Grades: 4.8, 8.8, 10.9, 12.9
  • Strength: 400–1220 兆帕
  • Surface: Zinc plated or galvanized
  • 最佳 for: 通用型 industrial use

Alloy Steel 12.9级

  • 等级: 12.9 (highest strength)
  • Tensile: Up to 1220 兆帕
  • Surface: Black oxide, zinc flake
  • 最佳 for: High-stress applications
