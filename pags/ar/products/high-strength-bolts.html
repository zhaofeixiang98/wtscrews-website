المواد & الدرجات

Grade 8.8

  • Tensile Strength: 800 MPa min
  • Yield Strength: 640 MPa min
  • فولاذ كربوني, quenched & tempered
  • General structural applications

Grade 10.9

  • Tensile Strength: 1000 MPa min
  • Yield Strength: 940 MPa min
  • Carbon or alloy steel
  • Heavy-duty structural use

Grade 12.9

  • Tensile Strength: 1220 MPa min
  • Yield Strength: 1100 MPa min
  • فولاذ سبيكي, heat treated
  • Critical high-stress applications

ASTM A325 / A490

  • ASTM A325 Type 1 (Carbon)
  • ASTM A325 Type 2 (Weathering)
  • ASTM A490 (Alloy, high strength)
  • Structural bolting specification
