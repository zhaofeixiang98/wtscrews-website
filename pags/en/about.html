About WT Fasteners

Your trusted partner in precision fastener manufacturing since 2005

Our Story

We started back in 2005 — just a small workshop, a handful of machines, and a simple idea: make fasteners that perform exactly as promised, every single time.

Over the past two decades we have grown into a full-scale operation with CNC machining, cold forging lines, and automated quality inspection. But the core idea has not changed. Every fastener that leaves our factory goes through the same rigorous checks we would expect if we were the ones using them.

Today we ship to over 50 countries. Our catalog covers thousands of specifications — standard hex bolts, flat washers, custom-engineered solutions for automotive, construction, and renewable energy projects. We hold ISO 9001 certification and stand behind every product we deliver.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom orders, the MOQ may vary depending on specifications — feel free to ask.

How long does production take?

Standard products: 7–15 business days. Custom orders: 15–25 business days depending on complexity and quantity.

Can you send samples before I place a bulk order?

Yes. We provide free samples for standard items (you cover shipping). For custom parts, a small sample fee may apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small orders. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Do you offer OEM / custom manufacturing?

Absolutely. Send us your drawings or specifications and we will provide a quote within 24 hours.
