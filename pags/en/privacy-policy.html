Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom orders, the MOQ may vary depending on specifications — feel free to ask.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes — we provide free samples for standard products. Freight is typically covered by the buyer, or we can deduct it from your first order.

What certifications do you have?

Our factory is ISO 9001 certified. We can supply material certificates, test reports, and certificates of conformity with every shipment.

How long is your lead time?

Standard items ship in 3–7 days. Custom orders typically take 15–30 days depending on quantity and specifications.

Can you do OEM / custom packaging?

Absolutely. We support custom branding, packaging, and labeling to match your requirements.
