Wood Screws

Our wood screws are engineered for exceptional holding power in wooden materials. Whether you are manufacturing furniture, building cabinetry, or working on construction projects, these screws provide reliable and secure fastening.

We offer various head styles and materials to meet different application requirements. All our wood screws feature sharp points for easy insertion and coarse threads for excellent grip in wood.

Materials & Grades

Brass Classic

  • Material: Brass (CuZn)
  • Corrosion resistance: Good
  • Appearance: Decorative golden finish
  • Best for: Fine furniture, decorative work

Stainless Steel 304 A2

  • Grade: A2-70 (700 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • Best for: Outdoor, moisture-prone areas

Stainless Steel 316 A4

  • Grade: A4-80 (800 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior
  • Marine grade available
  • Best for: Coastal, high-humidity

Carbon Steel Economy

  • Strength: Medium
  • Surface: Zinc or black phosphate
  • Cost-effective option
  • Best for: Indoor, general use
