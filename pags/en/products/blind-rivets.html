Blind Rivets

Our blind rivets (also known as pop rivets) are designed for one-sided installation where access to the back of the joint is not possible. The rivet is inserted and pulled (drawn) with a rivet tool, causing the back (blind side) to form a secure clamp.

Available in various materials and head styles to meet different application requirements. We also offer structural blind rivets for high-strength applications.
