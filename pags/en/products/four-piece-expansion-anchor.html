4-Piece Expansion Anchors (Four-Piece Wall Lizard)

Our 4-piece expansion anchors feature four independent expansion leaves that open evenly against the concrete wall, providing superior holding power compared to standard expansion bolts. Ideal for elevator rail installation, heavy machinery mounting, and structural anchoring. Manufactured to high-quality standards with consistent dimensional accuracy.

Our 4-piece expansion anchors are engineered for heavy-duty concrete anchoring applications. The unique four-leaf design ensures even radial expansion against the hole wall, distributing load uniformly and providing superior holding power compared to traditional two-piece or three-piece expansion anchors.

Available in carbon steel (zinc plated/black oxide) and stainless steel (304/316). Manufactured to precise dimensional standards. Each anchor features a cone nut that pulls back to expand the four leaves outward when the bolt is tightened, creating a secure mechanical interlock with the concrete.
