Nyloc Nuts

Our Nyloc nuts feature a nylon collar insert that grips the male thread, providing reliable locking action against vibration. The insert is reusable up to several times while maintaining its locking capability.

Perfect for applications where vibration loosening is a concern. Temperature range limited by nylon insert (-40°C to +120°C).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

How many times can a Nyloc nut be reused?

Nyloc nuts can typically be reused 5-10 times while maintaining adequate locking torque, depending on the application and thread condition.

What is the temperature range for Nyloc nuts?

Standard nylon insert: -40°C to +120°C. For high-temperature applications, we offer metal insert lock nuts (prevailing torque nuts).

Can Nyloc nuts be used with stainless steel bolts?

Yes, but we recommend using the same material grade to avoid galling. Stainless steel Nyloc nuts are ideal for use with stainless steel bolts.

Do you offer thin profile Nyloc nuts?

Yes, we offer both full height and thin profile versions per DIN 985 and ISO 10511 specifications.
