Cap Nut

Cap nuts feature a closed-end dome design that protects exposed bolt threads from damage, corrosion, and injury. Ideal for applications requiring safety, durability, and a clean decorative appearance. Available in various materials and finishes for industrial and outdoor use.

Cap nuts feature a closed-end dome design that protects exposed bolt threads from damage, corrosion, and external impact. The smooth rounded surface improves safety by preventing injuries and also provides a clean, decorative finish for visible applications.

Available in various sizes, materials, and surface finishes to meet different application requirements. Manufactured according to DIN 1587 / ISO standards with consistent dimensional accuracy and reliable performance in both indoor and outdoor environments.
