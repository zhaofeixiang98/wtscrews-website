Self Drilling Screws

Our self drilling screws, commonly known as "Tek" screws, feature a drill point that allows them to drill their own pilot hole and tap threads in one operation. This eliminates the need for pre-drilling, saving time and labor.

Available in various head styles and drill point types for different material thicknesses.
