U-Bolts

Our U-bolts are specifically designed for clamping and mounting pipes, tubes, and cylindrical objects. The U-shaped design wraps around the pipe while the threaded ends allow for secure fastening with nuts.

Available in both square bend and round bend configurations to meet different installation requirements. We offer various materials including carbon steel and stainless steel.
