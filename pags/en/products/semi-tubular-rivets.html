Semi-tubular Rivets

Our semi-tubular rivets feature a partial hollow shank (typically 50–70% of the shank length), combining the strength of solid rivets with the lighter weight and easier setting of tubular rivets. They are ideal for applications where a clinched (flowed) hole is acceptable.

Used extensively in the automotive, appliance, and furniture industries for sheet metal joining.
