shear-nuts

Our shear nuts (also known as break-away nuts or tamper-proof nuts) provide permanent, tamper-resistant fastening for public infrastructure, outdoor equipment, and security-critical applications. The hexagonal driving base shears off automatically once the installation torque is reached, leaving only a smooth conical head that cannot be gripped by standard tools. Manufactured in stainless steel 304/316 and zinc plated carbon steel for reliable corrosion resistance in indoor and outdoor environments.

Our shear nuts are engineered for applications where permanent, tamper-resistant fastening is essential. During installation, the hexagonal base provides a standard wrench grip for tightening. Once the preset torque is reached, the hex section shears cleanly away from the conical body, leaving a smooth dome profile that offers no purchase for conventional spanners, sockets, or pliers.

Widely used in public infrastructure, street furniture, outdoor enclosures, and any application where unauthorized removal must be prevented. Available in stainless steel 304/316 for corrosion-resistant outdoor use and zinc plated carbon steel for general applications. Each nut is manufactured to consistent torque breakaway specifications for predictable, reliable installation every time.
