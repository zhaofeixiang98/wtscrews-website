Hex Bolts

Our hex bolts are the backbone of countless industrial and construction projects worldwide. Whether you are assembling steel structures, heavy machinery, or automotive components, these bolts deliver consistent clamping force and long-term reliability.

We produce both full-thread (DIN 933 / ISO 4017) and partial-thread (DIN 931 / ISO 4014) variants. Every batch is tested for tensile strength, proof load, and dimensional accuracy before shipping.

Materials & Grades

Stainless Steel 304 A2

  • Grade: A2-70 (700 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • Best for: Food, chemical, marine environments

Stainless Steel 316 A4

  • Grade: A4-80 (800 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior (pitting resistant)
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • Best for: Chloride, acidic environments

Carbon Steel Class

  • Grades: 4.8, 8.8, 10.9, 12.9
  • Strength: 400–1220 MPa
  • Surface: Zinc plated or galvanized
  • Best for: General industrial use

Alloy Steel Grade 12.9

  • Grade: 12.9 (highest strength)
  • Tensile: Up to 1220 MPa
  • Surface: Black oxide, zinc flake
  • Best for: High-stress applications
