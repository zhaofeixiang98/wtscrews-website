High Strength Bolts

Our high strength structural bolts are designed for critical applications where maximum tensile and shear strength is required. Complying with international standards including ASTM A325, A490, and DIN 6914.

Heat-treated to achieve superior mechanical properties, these bolts are the standard choice for structural steel connections in construction, bridges, and industrial facilities.

Materials & Grades

Grade 8.8

Grade 10.9

  • Tensile Strength: 1000 MPa min
  • Yield Strength: 940 MPa min

  • Heavy-duty structural use

  • Critical high-stress applications
