Flat Washers

Our flat washers are the most common type of washer, used to distribute the load of a bolt or nut over a larger surface area. This prevents leakage, loosening, and surface damage.

Manufactured to DIN 125 / ISO 7089 standards with precise dimensional accuracy and consistent thickness.
