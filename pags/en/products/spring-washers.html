Ideal for high-stress applications, dynamic loads, and where space is at a premium.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

Can spring washers be stacked?

Yes, Belleville washers can be stacked in parallel or series to achieve different spring rates and load capacities.

What is the temperature range?

Carbon steel: -30°C to +120°C. Stainless steel: -200°C to +300°C. 50CrV4: up to +210°C.

What is the difference between Series A and B?

Series A is for stacked applications (bearing loads). Series B is for single use (low deflection).

Do you provide custom sizes?

Yes, we can manufacture custom sizes and specifications per your drawings.
