Eye Bolts

Our eye bolts feature a circular ring (eye) at one end, designed for vertical lifting and angular loads. Manufactured from high-strength alloy steel and 100% magnaflux tested for cracks. Available in both standard (DIN 580, for vertical lifting) and shoulder pattern (for angular loads) designs. Each bolt is load-rated with a minimum safety factor of 5:1.

Our eye bolts are engineered for secure lifting, towing, and anchoring applications. The forged one-piece construction from alloy steel eliminates the risk of weld failure. The eye (ring) is designed to accommodate hooks, shackles, or rope, providing a reliable pivot point for overhead lifts.

We offer two primary designs: Standard Eye Bolts (DIN 580) rated for vertical lifting only, and Shoulder Pattern Eye Bolts designed to withstand angular loads up to 45°. All products are proof-loaded and individually marked with thread size, load rating, and traceability code.
