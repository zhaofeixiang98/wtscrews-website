Wheel Bolts Assembly

Wheel bolt assemblies are critical fastening components used to secure wheels to vehicle hubs. Designed for high load-bearing performance, these bolts ensure safety and stability under dynamic driving conditions. Manufactured with precision machining and high-quality materials, our wheel bolts provide excellent tensile strength, wear resistance, and long service life. The assembly design (bolt + nut + washers) improves installation efficiency and fastening reliability. Suitable for a wide range of automotive applications, including passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and heavy-duty trucks
