Weld Nuts

Our weld nuts are designed for projection welding, creating a permanent bond to metal sheets and panels. The projections concentrate heat for strong, consistent welds in automated assembly.

Available in square and hexagonal designs. Ideal for automotive, appliance, and sheet metal assembly applications.
