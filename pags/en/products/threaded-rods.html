Threaded Rods

Our threaded rods (also known as all-thread or stud bolts) provide full-length threading along the entire shaft. They are versatile fasteners used for anchoring, suspending, and connecting in various industrial applications.

Available in metric and imperial sizes with various thread pitches. We offer both left-hand and right-hand thread options for specialized applications.

Materials & Grades

Stainless Steel 304 A2

  • Grade: A2-70 (700 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • Temperature range: -196°C to +800°C
  • Best for: Indoor, food industry

Stainless Steel 316 A4

  • Grade: A4-80 (800 MPa)
  • Corrosion resistance: Superior
  • Marine grade available
  • Best for: Outdoor, marine

Carbon Steel 8.8

  • Grade: 8.8 (800 MPa)
  • Surface: Zinc or galvanized
  • Cost-effective option
  • Best for: General industrial

Alloy Steel 10.9

  • Grade: 10.9 (1040 MPa)
  • High strength option
  • Black oxide finish
  • Best for: Heavy duty
