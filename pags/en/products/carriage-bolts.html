carriage-bolts

Our carriage bolts feature a smooth domed head and a square neck beneath that bites into the material upon installation, preventing rotation without the need for a wrench on the bolt side. Ideal for wood-to-wood, wood-to-metal, and general structural connections. Available in full-thread and partial-thread configurations. Manufactured to DIN 603 / ASME B18.5 standards with consistent dimensional accuracy.

Our carriage bolts feature a low-profile domed head and a square neck that embeds into the mating material upon tightening, providing a self-locking action that prevents the bolt from spinning during assembly. No wrench or tool is required on the bolt head side, making installation fast and clean on exposed surfaces.

Widely used in timber framing, playground equipment, fencing, furniture, and general structural assemblies. Available in full-thread and partial-thread versions, with a range of surface finishes to suit indoor and outdoor environments. Manufactured to DIN 603 standards with precise dimensional tolerances.
