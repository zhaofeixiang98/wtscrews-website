Dowel Pins

Our dowel pins (also known as parallel pins or locating pins) are precision-engineered cylindrical pins used for accurate alignment and positioning of mechanical components. The parallel sides ensure a precise fit in reamed holes.

Manufactured to DIN 7 and ISO 2339 standards with precision tolerances (m6 or h8). Available in various materials to suit different application requirements.
