Drywall Screws

Our drywall screws are specifically designed for gypsum board installation to metal studs or wood framing. The sharp bugle head sits flush with the drywall surface without tearing the paper facing.

We offer two thread types: fine thread for metal studs (25 gauge to 20 gauge) and coarse thread for wood framing. All our drywall screws feature black phosphate finish for superior corrosion resistance.

Materials & Grades

Carbon Steel Standard

  • Surface: Black Phosphate
  • Hardness: 530–680 HV
  • Corrosion resistance: Good
  • Best for: General construction

Carbon Steel Zinc

  • Surface: Zinc Plated
  • Hardness: 530–680 HV
  • Cost-effective option
  • Best for: Dry indoor applications

Stainless Steel 304 A2

  • Grade: A2-70
  • Corrosion resistance: Excellent
  • For high-humidity areas
  • Best for: Moisture-prone areas

Fine Thread Type S

  • For metal studs
  • 25–20 gauge capacity
  • More threads per inch
  • Best for: Steel framing
