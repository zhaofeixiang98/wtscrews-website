Solid Rivets

Our solid rivets (also known as buck rivets) are permanent fasteners used to join two or more pieces of material. The rivet is inserted into a pre-drilled hole and the tail (or shop end) is deformed using a rivet gun or hammer to create a secure, vibration-resistant joint.

Available in various materials including aluminum, steel, and stainless steel with different head styles to meet application requirements.
