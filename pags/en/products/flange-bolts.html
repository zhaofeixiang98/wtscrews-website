Flange Bolts

Our flange bolts feature an integrated circular washer under the head, distributing load evenly and preventing loosening under vibration. The serrated version creates a locking action against the mating surface.

Available in both serrated (vibration-resistant) and non-serrated configurations. Manufactured to DIN 6921 / ISO 1665 standards with consistent dimensional accuracy.
