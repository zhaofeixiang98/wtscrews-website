Expansion Bolts

Our expansion bolts (also known as sleeve anchors) are versatile anchors designed for use in concrete, brick, and block. The expansion sleeve expands against the base material as the bolt is tightened, creating a secure hold.

Available in various head styles including hex, round, and flat. Zinc-plated for indoor use and stainless steel for outdoor applications.
