Questions Fréquentes

Quelle est la quantité minimum de commande ?

Notre quantité minimum standard est de 10 kg par article. Pour les commandes sur mesure, le minimum peut varier selon les spécifications — n'hésitez pas à nous consulter.

What is the difference between Form A and Form B washers?

Form A is standard (un-chamfered), while Form B has chamfered edges. Both are available per DIN 125.

What hardness range do you offer?

Standard carbon steel washers have hardness HV 140-250. Higher hardness options available upon request.

Can you supply large diameter washers?

Oui, nous pouvons produce washers up to M64 and custom large diameters as per your specifications.

Do you offer special finishes?

Yes, we offer zingué, galvanisé à chaud, oxyde noir, and Passivation finishes.
