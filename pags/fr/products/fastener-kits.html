Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard Qté Minimum is 10 sets per item. For custom kits, the Qté Minimum may vary depending on contents — feel free to ask.

Can I customize the kit contents?

Yes, we offer fully customizable kits. Just tell us what items and quantities you need, and we will prepare a quote.

How long does production take?

Standard kits: 7–15 business days. Personnalisé kits: 15–25 business days depending on complexity and quantity.

What payment methods do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C, Western Union, and PayPal for small orders. We typically ask for 30% deposit with balance before shipment.

Do you offer OEM packaging with our brand?

Absolutely. We can customize packaging with your logo and branding. Contact us for more details.
