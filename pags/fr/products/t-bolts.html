Questions Fréquentes

Quelle est la quantité minimum de commande ?

Notre quantité minimum standard est de 10 kg par article. Pour les commandes sur mesure, le minimum peut varier selon les spécifications — n'hésitez pas à nous consulter.

What profile slot widths do you support?

We offer T bolts for slot widths from 6mm to 18mm, covering most standard profile systems.

Are these compatible with Bosch, Rexroth, and item profiles?

Yes, our T bolts are designed to be compatible with major European and Asian profile systems.

Do you offer different head styles?

Oui, nous pouvons provide standard T-head and low-profile versions per your requirements.

Can you supply custom lengths?

Oui, nous pouvons produce custom lengths from 15mm to 80mm based on your specifications.
