Questions Fréquentes

Quelle est la quantité minimum de commande ?

Notre quantité minimum standard est de 10 kg par article. Pour les commandes sur mesure, le minimum peut varier selon les spécifications — n'hésitez pas à nous consulter.

What is the difference between square and hexagonal weld nuts?

Square weld nuts (DIN 928) offer better positioning and higher shear strength. Hexagonal weld nuts (DIN 929) are easier to install with standard tools.

What welding method is used?

Projection weld nuts are designed for projection welding, where the projections concentrate heat for strong, consistent welds.

Can you supply stainless steel weld nuts?

Yes, we offer stainless steel 304 and 316 weld nuts. Note that stainless steel requires specific welding parameters.

Do you provide custom projections?

Oui, nous pouvons customize projection height and pattern for specific welding requirements.
