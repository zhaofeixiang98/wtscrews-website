Wie hoch ist die Mindestbestellmenge?

Unsere Standard-Mindestbestellmenge beträgt 10 kg pro Artikel. Bei Sonderanfertigungen kann die Mindestbestellmenge je nach Spezifikation variieren — fragen Sie gerne nach.

What profile slot widths do you support?

We offer T bolts for slot widths from 6mm to 18mm, covering most standard profile systems.

Are these compatible with Bosch, Rexroth, and item profiles?

Yes, our T bolts are designed to be compatible with major European and Asian profile systems.

Do you offer different head styles?

Ja, wir können provide standard T-head and low-profile versions per your requirements.

Can you supply custom lengths?

Ja, wir können produce custom lengths from 15mm to 80mm based on your specifications.
