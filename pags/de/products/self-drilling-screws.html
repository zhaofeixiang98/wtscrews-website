Selbstbohrschrauben

Unsere Selbstbohrschrauben (allgemein als "Tek" Schrauben bekannt) haben eine Bohrspitze, die es ihnen ermöglicht, ihr eigenes Führungsloch zu bohren und Gewinde in einer Operation zu schneiden.

Häufig gestellte Fragen

Wie hoch ist die Mindestbestellmenge?

Unsere Standard-Mindestbestellmenge beträgt 10 kg pro Artikel. Bei Sonderanfertigungen kann die Mindestbestellmenge je nach Spezifikation variieren — fragen Sie gerne nach.

What drilling capacity do self-drilling screws have?

Our self-drilling screws are available with drilling capacities ranging from 1.5mm to 6.0mm, depending on the screw size. The drill point effectively creates its own pilot hole while driving.

Are these screws suitable for metal-to-metal fastening?

Yes, self-drilling screws are specifically designed for metal-to-metal applications. The drill point penetrates the metal surface and the thread cuts into the material, creating a secure connection.

What head styles are available?

We offer multiple head styles including hex washer head, pan head, flat head, and countersunk head. Each style is designed for specific installation requirements and aesthetic preferences.

Do you provide custom coating options?

Yes, we offer various coating options including zinc plating, black phosphate, and stainless steel finishes. Custom coatings can be arranged for large orders based on your specific requirements.
