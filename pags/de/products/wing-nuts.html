Häufig gestellte Fragen

Wie hoch ist die Mindestbestellmenge?

Unsere Standard-Mindestbestellmenge beträgt 10 kg pro Artikel. Bei Sonderanfertigungen kann die Mindestbestellmenge je nach Spezifikation variieren — fragen Sie gerne nach.

Can wing nuts be used with power tools?

Wing nuts are designed for hand tightening. If power tool installation is needed, we recommend standard hex nuts or special adapter tools.

What materials are available?

We offer Kohlenstoffstahl (zinc or chrome plated), Edelstahl 304 and 316, and Messing.

Do you offer different wing styles?

Yes, we offer standard wing nuts, knurled wing nuts, and thumb nuts in various styles.

Are these suitable for outdoor use?

Edelstahl 316 wing nuts are ideal for outdoor and marine applications due to their corrosion resistance.
