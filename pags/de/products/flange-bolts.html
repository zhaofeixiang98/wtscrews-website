Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard MOQ is 10 kg per item. For custom orders, the MOQ may vary depending on specifications.

How long does production take?

Standard products: 7–15 business days. Custom orders: 15–25 business days depending on complexity.

Can you send samples?

Yes. We provide free samples for standard items (you cover shipping). For custom orders, samples can be provided with cost refunded on bulk order.

What surface finishes are available?

We offer Zinc Plated, Hot-Dip Galvanized, Black Oxide, Dacromet, and custom finishes upon request.

Do you offer serrated flange bolts?

Yes, we offer both serrated (vibration-resistant) and non-serrated versions. Serrated provides excellent locking action.
