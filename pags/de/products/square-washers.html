Häufig gestellte Fragen

Wie hoch ist die Mindestbestellmenge?

Unsere Standard-Mindestbestellmenge beträgt 10 kg pro Artikel. Bei Sonderanfertigungen kann die Mindestbestellmenge je nach Spezifikation variieren — fragen Sie gerne nach.

What sizes are available?

We offer square washers from M8 to M36, with custom sizes available upon request.

What is the advantage of square washers?

Square washers provide a larger bearing surface than round washers, distributing loads over a greater area - ideal for structural and wood applications.

Do you offer hot-dip galvanized finish?

Yes, we offer feuerverzinkt finish for outdoor and structural applications requiring enhanced corrosion resistance.

Can you supply custom dimensions?

Ja, wir können produce custom dimensions per your specifications for special applications.
