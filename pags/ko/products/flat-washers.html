What is the difference between Form A and Form B washers?

Form A is standard (un-chamfered), while Form B has chamfered edges. Both are available per DIN 125.

What hardness range do you offer?

표준 carbon steel washers have hardness HV 140-250. Higher hardness options available upon request.

Can you supply large diameter washers?

네, 가능합니다 produce washers up to M64 and custom large diameters as per your specifications.

Do you offer special finishes?

Yes, we offer 아연 도금, 용융 아연 도금, 흑색 산화, and 부동태화 finishes.
