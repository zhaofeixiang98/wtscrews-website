Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our 標準 最低注文 is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What is the difference between Form A and Form B washers?

Form A is 標準 (un-chamfered), while Form B has chamfered edges. Both are available per DIN 125.

What hardness range do you offer?

標準 carbon steel washers have hardness HV 140-250. Higher hardness options available upon request.

Can you supply large diameter washers?

はい、できます produce washers up to M64 and custom large diameters as per your specifications.

Do you offer special finishes?

Yes, we offer 亜鉛めっき, 溶融亜鉛めっき, 黒染め, and Passivation finishes.
