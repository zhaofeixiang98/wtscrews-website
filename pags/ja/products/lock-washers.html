よくある質問

最小注文数量はいくらですか？

標準最小注文数量は1品目あたり10kgです。カスタムオーダーの場合、仕様に応じて異なる場合があります。お気軽にお問い合わせください。

What is the difference between Type A and Type B lock washers?

Type A is the 標準 split lock washer. Type B has external teeth for additional locking in high-vibration applications.

What hardness are your lock washers?

Our 標準 carbon steel lock washers have hardness HV 440-510, providing optimal spring tension.

Can lock washers be reused?

Yes, lock washers can be reused several times, but the spring tension may decrease with each use.

Do you offer high-temperature lock washers?

Yes, we offer special high-temperature variants for applications above 200°C. お問い合わせ us for details.
