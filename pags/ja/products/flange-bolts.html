Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our 標準 最低注文 is 10 kg per item. For カスタム注文, the 最低注文 may vary depending on specifications.

How long does production take?

標準 products: 7–15 business days. カスタム orders: 15–25 business days depending on complexity.

Can you send samples?

Yes. We provide free samples for 標準 items (you cover shipping). For カスタム注文, samples can be provided with cost refunded on bulk order.

What surface finishes are available?

We offer 亜鉛めっき, 溶融亜鉛めっき, 黒染め, ダクロメット, and custom finishes upon request.

Do you offer serrated flange bolts?

Yes, we offer both serrated (vibration-resistant) and non-serrated versions. Serrated provides excellent locking action.
