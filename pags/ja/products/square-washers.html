What is the minimum order quantity?

Our 標準 最低注文 is 10 kg per item. For custom specifications, we can discuss based on your requirements.

What sizes are available?

We offer square washers from M8 to M36, with custom sizes available upon request.

What is the advantage of square washers?

Square washers provide a larger bearing surface than round washers, distributing loads over a greater area - ideal for structural and wood applications.
